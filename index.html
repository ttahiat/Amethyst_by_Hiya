About

As an artist, I specialize in capturing the essence of human emotions through my artwork, fostering connections and evoking deep feelings within my audience. Through my pieces, I delve into various social taboos and injustices, particularly focusing on issues concerning women. My art serves as a powerful medium for raising awareness and provoking thought, sparking conversations that lead to positive change.
